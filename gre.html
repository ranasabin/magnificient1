GRE – Graduate Record Examination

Students applying for graduate programs (Master’s or Ph.D.) in the USA may need to take the GRE, in addition to the TOEFL. The requirement depends on your chosen field of study and the universities to which you are applying.

There are two types of GRE exams: GRE General Test and GRE Subject Tests. The General Test is computer-based and includes analytical writing, verbal reasoning, and quantitative reasoning. It is available on most business days at USEF. The Subject Tests are paper-based and conducted approximately three times a year at St. Xavier’s School.

Test Overview

  • Test Dates: Check the testing calendar at www.ets.org/gre
  • Cost: USD 220
  • Register: By mail via dollar-denominated bank draft or online with an international credit card. Refer to the bulletin or website for details.
  • Website: www.ets.org/gre
