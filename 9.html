  • Passport: Valid for at least 3 months beyond your departure date.

  • Proof of Funds: Bank statements, sponsor's financial documents, or scholarship proof.
  • Tuition Fee Receipt: Proof of payment or fund availability.

  • Medical Certificates: Required for stays over 6 months.
  • Police Clearance: For stays longer than 24 months.
  • Photographs: Two recent passport-sized photos.
  • SOP: Statement of Purpose explaining your education plans.
  • Academic Documents: Transcripts and certificates.
  • English Proficiency: IELTS/TOEFL scores unless exempt.
