Scholarships for International Students

Denmark offers various scholarship opportunities for students from around the world. Here are the most notable options:

1. Danish Government Scholarships

Available to highly qualified non-EU/EEA students via Danish universities. Covers full or partial tuition and sometimes living costs.

2. Erasmus+ Program

Supports EU/EEA students in exchange programs in Denmark. Covers tuition and living expenses.

3. Nordplus

Offers student exchange and funding for students from Nordic and Baltic countries studying in Denmark.

4. University-Specific Scholarships

Most Danish universities provide merit-based scholarships for international students, including tuition waivers and living allowances.

How to Apply for Scholarships

  1. Research: Visit university and government websites to explore available scholarships.
  2. Prepare Documents: Include transcripts, CV, SOP, English proof, and recommendation letters tailored for each scholarship.
  3. Apply Early: Meet deadlines well in advance to ensure a complete and successful application.
  4. Seek Assistance: Contact international offices or scholarship coordinators for support and clarification.
