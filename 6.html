If you’re a Nepalese student planning to study in Ireland, securing your D-Study Visa is a crucial step. Below is a verified and updated checklist based on guidelines from the Irish Immigration Service and VFS Global Ireland.

  1. Visa Fees and VFS Charges
    Single Entry: €60 (~NPR 8,500)
    Multiple Entry: €100 (~NPR 14,200)
    VFS Service Charge: NPR 4,420
    Payment: Pay visa fee via AVATS online; VFS fee at center.
  2. Passport
    Valid for at least 12 months beyond arrival; must have 2 blank pages.
    Biometric data required (fingerprints/photo) at VFS Kathmandu.
  3. Passport-Sized Photographs
    Two recent photos. On the back, sign and write your AVATS VATN number.
  4. Private Medical Insurance
    Must be valid for entire stay. May be arranged by your college or yourself.
  5. Application Letter
    Include personal details, study plans, travel dates, family in EU, and visa compliance commitment.
  6. Previous Visa Refusals
    If any, provide details and original refusal letter.
  7. Letter of Acceptance from Your College
    Must confirm full-time course, duration, fees, and insurance (if included).
  8. Account for Gaps in Education
    Explain gaps and include employment history if applicable.
  9. Proof of Fee Payment
    Include ETF receipts or approved service proof. Rules:
    - Pay full if < €6,000
    - Pay at least €6,000 if above that
  10. Evidence of Academic Ability
    Include results, transcripts, and qualifications.
  11. Financial Requirements
    Prove €10,000 for first year living + tuition.
    For later years, show €10,000 + tuition.
    Use official bank documents.
  12. Evidence of English Language Ability
    IELTS or equivalent test results.
  13. Summary of Finances
    Fill out financial summary form proving self-sufficiency.
  14. Accommodation & Vetting Certificate
    Provide intended address in Ireland.
    Vetting (police clearance) may be needed.
  15. VFS Global Application Process
    Submit via VFS website or Kathmandu office. Pay fees and schedule biometric appointment.
  16. Additional Requirements
    Interview may be required. Additional proof of ties to Nepal or travel history might be needed.

Tips for Nepalese Students

  • Apply early (4–8 week processing time).
  • Ensure all documents are accurate and complete.
  • Follow current Irish Immigration and VFS guidelines.
