Post-Study Work Visa Options

  • Post-Study Work Visa (Employer Assisted): Allows graduates to work for any employer in a role related to their qualification for up to two years.
  • Post-Study Employer Accreditation: For graduates with a job offer from an accredited employer, which may lead to residency.
  • Skilled Migrant Category (Residence): Offers residency based on qualifications, work experience, and job offer.

How to Secure a Job Post-Study

  • Networking via university events, LinkedIn, and industry expos
  • Use job platforms like Seek, Trade Me Jobs, and Indeed
  • Attend career fairs and expos
  • Tailor your CV and cover letter to New Zealand standards

Top Post-Study Job Opportunities

  • Information Technology: Software development, cybersecurity
  • Healthcare: Nursing, physiotherapy, medical lab tech
  • Engineering: Civil, mechanical, electrical
  • Hospitality and Tourism: Hotels, restaurants, adventure tourism

Working in New Zealand: What to Expect

  • Hourly Rates: Minimum wage as of 2024 is around NZD $21.20/hour
  • Work Culture: Known for work-life balance and employee well-being
  • Job Portals: JobKiwi, Workhere NZ, and others

For international students looking to kickstart their careers in a vibrant and welcoming environment, New Zealand presents an array of post-study work opportunities. From securing the right visa to exploring job prospects and experiencing a fulfilling work-life balance, New Zealand promises a rewarding journey.
